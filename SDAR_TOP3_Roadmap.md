## Strategic context

**Domain:** samedayappliance.repair, DR 48 (very high for niche)
**Niche structure:** 5 directions (residential, commercial kitchen, commercial refrigeration, ice machines, outdoor)
**SERP reality:** competitors mostly DR 0–26. Top-3 achievable in 2–8 weeks per page when quality bar applied.
**Existing footprint:** ~600 pages, mostly residential brand × service combos. Hubs for new niches just stubbed.

**Sequencing logic:**
1. **Outdoor first** — completely empty SERP, KD 0 across the board, highest ROI per page written. Premium audience (Beverly Hills, Malibu, Bel Air) = highest ticket.
2. **Commercial Refrigeration second** — already have GSC footprint (positions 5–11 for `commercial refrigeration repair cost`, `commercial ice machine repair cost`). Strengthen what's ranking.
3. **Ice Machines third** — separate intent (bars/hotels/restaurants), 20+ brand pages already exist but no hub structure.
4. **Commercial Kitchen expansion fourth** — fill gaps (mixers, slicers, combi ovens, steamers).
5. **Residential gaps last** — already mature, lowest marginal ROI per page.
